About this edition

The volume

Babken Chookaszian (Բաբկէն Չուգասզեան, 1923 Tabriz — 5 November 1997 Yerevan) was an Armenian philologist and orientalist, Deputy Director of the Matenadaran from 1965 to 1994. This edition presents Volume I of his collected articles, published in Yerevan 2022/2023 as a second expanded edition. The printed volume runs 573 pages: 550 pages of scholarly text (55 articles + back matter) plus a 23-page plates insert of family portraits and Matenadaran miniatures.

Scope of the digital edition

This is not a facsimile. It is a full scholarly digital edition: extraction, structure recovery, TEI P5 encoding, entity enrichment, IIIF v3 image delivery, and citation-grounded retrieval. Everything is regenerated from source on every commit; nothing is hand-edited HTML.

  • Every article is TEI P5, validated against RelaxNG and Schematron
  • Every printed page break is marked with <pb n="P"/>
  • Persons and manuscripts are auto-extracted with explicit provenance
  • Plates are IIIF v3 with a manuscript register cross-linked from each Canvas
  • The "Ask the corpus" retrieval endpoint refuses to answer without citations
